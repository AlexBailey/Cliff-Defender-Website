Welcome to the official website of the Cliff Defender game, this site will allow you to download the game, keep up to date with recent news and also will let you know more about the game in general. Cliff Defender is 2D Tower Defence game with a subliminal educational message behind the initial impression of the game which is aimed to encourage junior school aged children to take care of the environment and wildlife.

Watch out! These are the enemies that can destroy the cliff, you must defend them away!

This is "Sea", Sea will try and break down the cliff with huge waves and other things!


This is "Wind", Wind will try and break down the cliff with huge gusts of wind, tornados and other things!
